Omiš — land, river & sea

Where the Cetina cuts through the mountains before meeting the Adriatic, you can run buggy and quad trails in the hills, then switch to SUP, kayak, or a boat on the water — all from one town. Local crews know the routes, the wind, and the quiet spots worth stopping for.
