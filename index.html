Cetina kayak rent

Self-Guided River Rental Adventure

Take the wheel and explore at your own pace. The most flexible way to discover the beauty of the Cetina River with a partner.

Tour overview

Discover the serenity of the Cetina River with our flexible 4-hour self-guided kayak rental. Important: each rental kayak holds a maximum of 2 people—one boat, two seats. Perfect for couples or friends, you will pilot a stable two-person boat through calm waters. Take control of your journey by paddling at your own speed, pausing for a refreshing swim, and returning to our base where your adventure began.

Difficulty Very easy
